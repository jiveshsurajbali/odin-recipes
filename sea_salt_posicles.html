Sea Salt Popsicles

Description

This recipe is taken from the Kingdom Hearts series of games, which are based on the characters of the Disney universe. It is a simple but delicious treat to prepare. It is the perfect combination of both sweet and salty. The popsicles should be prepared well in advance of when it will be served, as it needs time in the freezer to set. The quantity of salt is not specified as it is subjective to taste. Experiment with different batches to find the perfect balance.

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2cup honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • sea salt
  • 13 drops blue food colouring
  • 3 drops green food colouring

      • Steps

    1. In a saucepan, over medium-high heat, combine the heavy cream, milk and honey.
    2. Once combined remove the saucepan from the heat and add the vanilla extract.
    3. Pour the mixture in a bowl and slowly add the sea salt. Taste until you are happy with the level of saltiness. It is easy to overdo it so be careful.
    4. Finally, add the food coloring. Experiment with colouring to match the game likeness or to try new colours.
    5. Place in the refrigerator and allow it to chill for 2 hours.
    6. After it has cooled, place the cream mixture in the molds and allow to chill in the freezer overnight.
    7. Enjoy!
